# DPDP + GDPR Compliance Checker  
  
**Live Tool:** [https://uditmalikai.github.io/dpdp-compliance-checker/](https://uditmalikai.github.io/dpdp-compliance-checker/)  
  
A no-BS, browser-based compliance tester for websites and online programmes targeting Indian and European users.  
  
Evaluates against:  
- **India's DPDP Act 2023 + DPDP Rules 2025**  
- **EU GDPR (Regulation 2016/679)**  
  
Zero backend. Zero tracking. Pure client-side HTML/CSS/JS.  
  
---  
  
## What It Does  
  
- **Scores your site 0–100** across DPDP and GDPR  
- **Section breakdowns**: Notice & Consent, Rights & DPO, Security & Breach, Cross-Border & Special Data  
- **Instant recommendations**: Tailored legal fixes based on your answers  
- **Dual tabs**: Switch between DPDP (India) and GDPR (EU) questions  
- **Visual feedback**: Animated score ring, color-coded risk levels (Good / Needs Improvement / High Risk)  
  
---  
  
## Who It's For  
  
- Universities, gov programmes, SaaS, fintech, health platforms  
- Legal, compliance, DPO teams needing a fast status check  
- Product/marketing teams doing pre-launch privacy audits  
- Anyone who wants to stop guessing and start measuring  
  
---  
  
## How to Use  
  
1. Open [the tool](https://uditmalikai.github.io/dpdp-compliance-checker/)  
2. Enter your website URL and org type  
3. Answer DPDP questions (tab 1) and GDPR questions (tab 2)  
4. Click **"Calculate Compliance"**  
5. Get your score + tailored legal recommendations  
6. Share the URL with your team
